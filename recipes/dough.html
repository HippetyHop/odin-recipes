Mama's Pizza dough and sauce

Description

Recipe that my mother, a pizza master, gave me once I moved to abroad.

I always kept it close to my heart but in honor of her birthday, I've decided to share it with you.

Enjoy and Bon Appetit!

Ingridients

  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 kg flour
  • 2 Tbsp oil
  • 1 dl water
  • tsp sugar
  • yeast

Steps

  1. Make a hole in flour
  2. Add yeast, sugar a water, let it raise
  3. Add oli, salt and the rest of water
  4. Mix flour until it's medium hard, let it raise
  5. Shape it into balls and let them raise as well
